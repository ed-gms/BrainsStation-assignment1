Biography

Galactic Emperors is a Canadian rock band from Vancouver, British Columbia, formed in 1998. The band consists of Levi Broadland (lead vocals, guitar, bass, keyboards), Everett James (drums, percussion) and Connor Winde (lead guitar, bass, violin, keyboards, other instruments).

The band's debut album, Galactic Emperors (2001), was produced by Milos Price and released to positive reviews but only to limited commercial success. The band achieved worldwide recognition with the release of their second album Mayhem in Mercury (2004), which received numerous endorsements all over the world, including platinum in the Australia and Sweden. Their next release, Venus Vikings (2009), showed a vivid evolution in the band's musical style, as they merged experimental music with diverse inspirations. Following a year stay in Budapest, Galactic Emperors quickly released their fourth album, Mars Musings (2007) which was met with critical and commercial success. As of September 2008, the band had sold over 20 million albums worldwide. Galactic Emperors went on with much success in their release of other albums such as Jurisdiction Jupiter (2009), Saturn Stereochemistry (2012), Uranus Unbound (2015) and most recently, Notions of Neptune (2017).
